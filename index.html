About Me

My name is Corey Yates. I received a Bachelor's Degree in US History from the Univesity of California at Berkeley in 2006. My love of history, women and fashion lead me to start a business in 1999 that sells vintage sewing patterns. Our business has grown from an Ebay business to Etsy and our own website, Vintage4me2.com.

I am looking forward to using this class to grow both my business and my proffessional resume to work on websites for other small businesses. I have mastered important programs and web applications like Shopify and Mailchimp. I run successful email and social media campaigns to keep people interested in Vintage Sewing.

When we started this business in 1999, the web was very different than today, we helped to grow a niche market in Vintage Patterns into a fun and vibrant corner of the web. Please join my facebook group to get a sense of the world of vintage sewing patterns
